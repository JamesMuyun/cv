Research Associate

Biography

I am currently working as a Research Associate in ST Engineering-NTU Corporate Laboratory at NTU Singapore under supervision by Prof. Danwei Wang. I am working on improving UAV navigation performance using Thermal Camera in low light urban environment, multi sensor calibration as well as implementing Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO) algorithms. My area of research also lies in Data-Driven time serial data analysis for Fault Detection and Fault Diagnosis.
